# Pokemon-Fractured-Realms
A dimensional collapse merges three regions into one unstable world, pulling elements from Sinnoh (Platinum) and Kalos (Y) into a fractured Hoenn-based overworld.

STORYLINE (Platinum + Y Spinoff)

After the events of Platinum and Y, distortions caused by:
	•	Giratina (Distortion World instability)
	•	AZ’s Ultimate Weapon (residual energy)

…tear open dimensional rifts across reality.

You are:

A trainer recruited by an interregional task force studying “Fracture Zones.”

⸻

Main Plot Beats

ACT 1 – Hoenn (Starting Region)
	•	Start in Littleroot (but altered)
	•	Professor Birch studies “Anomaly Pokémon”
	•	First encounters include regional variants and Gen 6–9 Pokémon

Villain Group: Team Paradox
	•	Goal: Control rifts to rewrite reality

⸻

ACT 2 – Sinnoh Fragment (Platinum Influence)
	•	Accessed via distortion portals
	•	Snowpoint + Distortion World hybrid maps
	•	Encounter:
	•	Giratina (Origin Forme)
	•	Dialga/Palkia anomalies

⸻

ACT 3 – Kalos Fragment (Y Influence)
	•	Lumiose City partially reconstructed
	•	Mega Evolution becomes central mechanic
	•	AZ appears as a key NPC guiding the player

⸻

FINAL ACT
	•	Reality destabilizes
	•	All 3 regions overlap into a chaotic “Fracture Nexus”
	•	Final boss:
	•	Paradox Giratina (new form)
	•	Followed by Team Paradox leader using Mega + Legendary fusion team
